ABOUT GATHR LABS
Gathr Labs is an up-and-coming web development studio based on the Sunshine Coast. We have a strong focus on using advanced web development techniques. We craft quality digital products and shy away from pre-made templates and themes. Everything we build is unique and hand-made for you.
HOW WE WORK
Gathr Labs has experience working across multiple languages, frameworks, libraries and APIs in order to deliver a complete web development package. Together we can build your next responsive site. e-store. platform. web app.

  • forumConsultation

    Perhaps the most important part of the entire process. We talk - either in person or remotely - so that we can get an understanding of what you want out of your digital product. Guided by some key questions, this is our opportunity to understand your ideas, gather what inspires you and nail down important items such as brand identity, design and what you want out of your digital product.

  • settings_brightnessPrototyping

    Following our consultation, we'll have a good idea about what you want. Now we take all of the information and create a design prototype using tools such as Sketch 3, Principle and Flinto. This will enable you to see the front-end design of your product very quickly including animations and the full user-interface. Providing a prototype allow us to iron out any design issues at the outset, ultimately saving time in the development segment.

  • codeDevelopment

    The fun part; we make your design into a full working product! We set you up with a domain and secure hosting on Australian servers. Then we start coding. Your design is completely custom built with html, CSS and JS, no themes are used. We pride ourselves on this. Depending on your product specifications, we may convert your site into a Wordpress or Drupal site or build an entirely custom content management system.

  • trending_upAnalytics

    We don't just get up and leave once a site is finished. We hand the controls back over to you with a full run-down of how to manage your product. We will help you monitor your site's activity and optimise it so that it can be seen by as many people as possible. We employ the use of google analytics and other tools to track visitors and A/B split testing to test different site components to see which performs better.
