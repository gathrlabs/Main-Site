ABOUT GATHR LABS
Gathr Labs is a web development studio based on the Sunshine Coast. Founded by Tom Scerri in 2016, my goal is to build digital products and services that bring actual value to people, ones that solve a problem and make life a little bit easier.
I base the strength of a site on its usability, intuitiveness and speed. My mantra is to keep it clean and simple. All of my work is tailored to my client's specificaitons. I don't rely on cookie-cutter wordpress or squarespace templates. I build everything myself to make sure your digital prescence is one of a kind.
HOW WE WORK
I have experience working across multiple languages, frameworks, libraries and APIs in order to deliver a complete web development package. I will build your next responsive website. e-commerce store. web platform. web application.

  • forumConsultation

    Perhaps the most important part of the entire process. We talk - either in person or remotely - so that I can get an understanding of what you want out of your digital product. Guided by some key questions, this is my opportunity to understand your ideas, gather what inspires you and nail down important items such as brand identity, design and what you want out of your digital product.

  • settings_brightnessPrototyping

    Following our consultation, I'll have a good idea about what you want. Now I take all of the information and create a design prototype using tools such as Sketch 3, Principle and Flinto. This will enable you to see the front-end design of your product very quickly including animations and the full user-interface. Providing a prototype allow us to iron out any design issues at the outset, ultimately saving time in the development segment.

  • codeDevelopment

    The fun part; we make your design into a full working product! We set you up with a domain and secure hosting on Australian servers. Then we start coding. Your design is completely custom built with html, CSS and JS, no themes are used. We pride ourselves on this. Depending on your product specifications, we may convert your site into a Wordpress or Drupal site or build an entirely custom content management system.

  • trending_upAnalytics

    I don't just get up and leave once a site is finished. I hand the controls back over to you with a full run-down of how to manage your product. I will help you monitor your site's activity and optimise it so that it can be seen by as many people as possible. I employ the use of google analytics and other tools to track visitors and A/B split testing to test different site components to see which performs better.
