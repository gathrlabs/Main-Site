ABOUT GATHR LABS
Gathr Labs is a web development studio based on the Sunshine Coast. Founded by Tom Scerri in 2015, my goal is to build digital products and services that bring actual value to people, ones that solve a problem and make life a little bit easier.
I base the strength of a site on its usability, intuitiveness and speed. My mantra is to keep it clean and simple. All of my work is tailored to my client's specificaitons. I don't rely on cookie-cutter wordpress or squarespace themes. I build everything myself to make sure your digital prescence is one of a kind.
